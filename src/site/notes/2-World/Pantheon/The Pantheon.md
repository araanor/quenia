The pantheon is composed of gods and goddesses some of whom are humanoid and others just celestial concepts. they can often manifest themselves into the mortal plane to have fun or help civilization.
Many of them have had human relationships due to their close resemblance. Most notable ones are [[2-World/Pantheon/Mephestus, Hierarch of Hell & Death Herself\|Mephestus]] and [[2-World/Pantheon/Aijacs, All-seeing Judge & Celestial Sovereign\| Aijacs]]. 
There are also other less notable beings that aren't worshiped very commonly but still hold some cosmic power.

# The Primary Pantheon is:
1. [[2-World/Pantheon/Aijacs, All-seeing Judge & Celestial Sovereign\|Aijacs, All-seeing Judge & Celestial Sovereign]]
2. [[2-World/Pantheon/Mephestus, Hierarch of Hell & Death Herself\|Mephestus, Hierarch of Hell & Death Herself]]
3. [[2-World/Pantheon/Ardon, Fire Bringer\|Ardon, Fire Bringer]]
