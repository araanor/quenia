# Resting
> [!abstract] Short rest
>  A short rest can occur when there is no combat and space to sleep. The rest lasts 2 hours.

> [!abstract] Long rest
> A long rest is composed of 3 short rests lasting 6 hours total. During a long rest you must spend 4 hours sleeping and can spend 2 hours doing light work (studying, watch, eating).

# Actions
> [!abstract] Cantrips
>  Cantrips count as a bonus action instead of a regular action.

> [!abstract] Health Potions
> You can drink or feed another player a Health potion as a bonus action however you have to roll to see how many Health points it restores. If you drink it as an action then you get the max number of points.
