# INTRO
Born on the 17th of January 1307, in [[2-World/Anthor/Malia, Republic of/Malia, Republic of\|Malia]], Rayyo was attacked as a child and him and his family fled to [[2-World/Anthor/Atenian Empire/Atenian Empire\|Atenia]] where he lived for the next few years. He gained powers through a sword he gathered during his travels across [[2-World/Anthor/Anthor\|Anthor]].
